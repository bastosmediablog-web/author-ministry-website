Our Ministry

Serving God • Serving People • Transforming Lives

Our Mission

Great Commission Christian Church exists to glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, making disciples, nurturing believers in biblical truth, and demonstrating God's love through worship, prayer, and compassionate outreach.

🙏 Worship Services

Experience heartfelt worship where believers gather to honor God through praise, prayer, and biblical teaching.

📖 Bible Teaching

Growing spiritually through sound biblical doctrine and practical teaching for everyday Christian living.

❤️ Community Outreach

Serving individuals and families through acts of compassion, evangelism, and community support.

🌍 Missions

Taking the message of Jesus Christ beyond our community to impact lives around the world.

🙏 Prayer Ministry

Believing in the power of prayer to bring healing, hope, restoration, and spiritual breakthrough.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Ministry

Helping strengthen marriages, families, youth, and children through biblical principles.
