About Rev. Dr. Jacob Kehinde Oladipupo

Pastor • Author • Teacher • Conference Speaker

A Life Dedicated to Christ

Rev. Dr. Jacob Kehinde Oladipupo is a devoted servant of Jesus Christ whose passion is to proclaim the Gospel, disciple believers, and equip the Church through biblical teaching.

As the founder and pastor of Great Commission Christian Church, he has faithfully served individuals and families by preaching God's Word with clarity, compassion, and conviction.

Beyond the pulpit, Rev. Dr. Oladipupo is an accomplished Christian author whose books inspire readers to deepen their relationship with Christ, strengthen their faith, and live according to biblical principles.

His ministry continues to impact lives through preaching, teaching, conferences, community outreach, and Christian literature that reaches readers around the world.

Our Vision

To glorify God by making disciples, teaching biblical truth, strengthening families, and reaching the nations with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
